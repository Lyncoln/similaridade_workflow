Prioritize gene list

Created: 2015-04-03 20:51:11
  • Download Workflow

This workflow prioritizes a gene list according to its association with the 'concept_id'. In our example here we are prioritizing a gene list to obtain genes that are more closely associated to huntingtin, the cause of huntington's disease

Inputs (3)
Name Description
gene_IDs a comma separated gene list to prioritized
database_name The ID for the database that will be used to match the gene list and obtain their concept ids. Here we used entrez gene ids, and therefor db_name = EG
concept_id id of the concept to match the gene list against, entrez gene ids in the case where database_name = EG In our case we use the entrez gene identifier to match our gene list against the huntingtin protein
